Lasagna Recipe

Description

This dish creates a layered lasagna with premium italian. Tops with metled italian cheese and mozzarella.

In the words of Post Malone, this dish will have you sayin "Wow."

Ingredients

  • Package of Lasagna Noodles?
  • 1 Cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 Cup Italian Cheese
  • 2 lbs Italian Sausage
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • 1 Cup Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook Italian Sausage Medium Heat
  2. Once Brown Put Half of Sauage in Pan
  3. Layer Sheet of Lasagna Noodles on top
  4. Pour Cheese on Top
  5. Repeat last three steps
  6. Once to the brim, pour that sauce on it!!!
